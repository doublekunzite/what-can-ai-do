Which Model Should I Use?

"I need to analyze a 100-page PDF"

Use: Claude 3.5 Sonnet (200K token context)

"I want the best coding help"

Use: GPT-4o or Claude 3.5 Sonnet

"I need something free for basic tasks"

Use: Gemini 1.5 Flash or free tier of GPT-4o

"I'm concerned about privacy"

Use: Llama 3 running locally on your computer

"I need the fastest response"

Use: Gemini 1.5 Flash or smaller models

Understanding Benchmarks

MMLU (Massive Multitask Language Understanding)

Tests knowledge across 57 subjects (math, history, law, etc.). Higher score = broader knowledge.

HumanEval

Measures coding ability. Tests if model can write correct code from descriptions.

Important Caveat

Benchmarks don't capture real-world usefulness. A model with slightly lower scores might be better for your specific task. Always test with your actual use case.

Free vs. Paid: What's the Difference?

Free Tiers

  • Rate limits (messages per hour)
  • May use older models
  • Your data might be used for training

Paid Versions ($20/month)

  • Higher rate limits
  • Access to best models
  • Option to disable training data usage
  • Priority during high-traffic periods
