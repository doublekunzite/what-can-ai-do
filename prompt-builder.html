30-Minute Study Session Builder

Create a structured learning workflow with an AI tutor.

Conversation Flow:

Step 1: Foundation (5 min)

I need to study {{subject}} at a {{level}} level. 
First, create a concise study guide that covers the key concepts I must understand. 
Include 3-5 main topics with brief descriptions.

Step 2: Active Recall (10 min)

Based on the study guide you just provided, generate 10 practice questions about {{subject}}. 
Mix question types: 4 definition/fact-based, 3 application problems, and 3 "explain in your own words" prompts. 
Don't provide the answers yet‚ÄîI'll answer them first.

Step 3: Check & Explain (10 min)

Here are my answers to your questions: [PASTE YOUR ANSWERS HERE]
                                
Grade my responses and explain what I got wrong or could improve. 
For each incorrect answer, provide a detailed explanation and a mnemonic to help me remember it.

Step 4: Connection & Application (5 min)

Now connect {{subject}} to real-world applications or current research. 
Give me 2 examples of how this is used in practice, and 1 area where our understanding is still developing.

Research Project Assistant

Build a comprehensive research workflow.

Step 1: Research Plan

I need to research "{{topic}}". 
First, help me create a research plan. What are the 5-7 key sub-topics I should investigate? 
For each, suggest 2-3 search terms and 1-2 types of sources to look for.

Step 2: Source Evaluation

I've found {{sources}} sources. Help me evaluate them:
1. Sort them by credibility (peer-reviewed > academic > government > NGO > news > blog)
2. Identify 3 sources that might have bias and explain what to watch for
3. Suggest which 5-7 sources I should prioritize reading first

Step 3: Synthesis

Based on these sources about "{{topic}}", create a synthesis matrix:
- 3 main schools of thought or approaches
- 2 areas of consensus across sources
- 3 major debates or disagreements
- 4 gaps in current research
